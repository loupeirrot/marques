Frequently asked questions

How do I start the process of buying a home?
The first step is to determine your budget and financing options. If you need a mortgage, getting pre-approved can help you understand how much you can afford. Once you're ready, our real estate experts will assist you in finding properties that meet your needs.
How can i start a project with Architect?
The process begins with a consultation where we discuss your vision, goals, and budget. Once we understand your needs, our team will prepare a proposal outlining the project scope, estimated timeline, and costs before moving forward.
What services does the studio offer?
We provide a full range of architectural and design services — from concept development and 3D visualization to construction management and interior design. Our goal is to deliver cohesive and creative solutions tailored to each client.
How long does it take to complete a project?
Project timelines vary depending on scale and complexity. Smaller residential projects may take a few months, while larger commercial developments can extend to a year or more. We’ll provide a detailed schedule after the initial consultation.
Do you offer advice on sustainable design?
Absolutely. We integrate eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient systems, and sustainable construction practices into our designs whenever possible. Our team can also guide you through certifications like LEED to make your project environmentally responsible.
